Premise

OpenEnded is a top-down shooter game. With local multiplayer support, players can play side-by-side on the same computer to clear the world of enemies. Players can fight enemies with different types of weapons including projectiles, swords, and leaving behind mines. They can explore the infinite-everchanging world full of maze like structures and rectangular ruins.

Development

  • Made with Java and the Java Swing Library
  • Personal game developed by myself since my first programming class.
  • Inspired by Dungeons of Fayte
