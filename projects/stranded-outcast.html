Premise

Your plane crashes onto a small uninhabited island. With no people to communicate with and no nearby civilization to fall back on, you are an Outcast. Soon, you discover the reason the island is abandoned and uninhabited: at night, animals that seem to appear out of nowhere deviate from their docile and minimally territorial ways to hunt you down. You, the Outcast, have only one option. The East shore of the island has a small dock with a boat in need of repair, perhaps left from the previous inhabitants of the island. Your goal is now to survive on this haunted island, collecting enough resources to repair the boat and escape!
