Premise

Become the best at snowball fights! Gather snow from snow piles, build snow walls to duck behind, throw snow balls at others. Rise up to the top of the leaderboard. Just try not to get hit!

IO games are multiplayer server-based games that involve many players fighting for the top of the leaderboard. A popular example is Agar.io which is credited for popularizing this genre of games.

Snowfight.io is an io game where players try to get the most hits off of other players by throwing snowballs. Play against friends and family to find out who is best.

Development

  • Founded a company called eyeoh.io with four other friends
  • Made as a game for our company website
  • Simple AI to fill empty server
