Premise

During quarantine do to COVID19, students who need to participate in campus activities, live in San Luis Obispo, or on the Cal Poly campus have to take COVID tests, self-screeners to show that they do not have any COVID symptoms, and check their test results. Each of these requires a different process to set up and a different website to go to. The idea behind iaso is to bring all of these together into a single app for students to do all of these required activities. Iaso is also intended to include tips on how to slow the spread of COVID and help keep students up to date in the most recent news regarding COVID.
